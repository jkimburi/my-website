J.K


Jose-Maria Kimburi is an 18-year-old author & filmmaker based in the UK. His work explores both human and physical change through geography, with creative projects centred around people-place relationships.

18 Reflections: Divides, Epidemics and the Generation Caught in Between is his debut book, written to articulate the complex realities his generation faces as they grow up amid social, environmental, economic and health challenges.

BioVerse TV: Unplugging from Social Media is his debut documentary, encouraging people to step back from the constant busyness of digital life and reconnect with the natural world. The film highlights the mental health benefits of spending time in nature while showcasing its beauty and biodiversity. By weaving together personal experiences, expert interviews and practical advice, BioVerse TV presents a refreshing and accessible way to rethink our relationship with both technology and the environment.

Both his debut book and debut documentary will be released in the coming months.
